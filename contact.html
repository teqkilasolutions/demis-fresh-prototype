Frequently Asked Questions

Can I drink from this pack immediately?

Yes, but the best way to enjoy any Demi’s product is to serve it chilled. Also, always remember to shake the pack well before use.

How long can I use this pack once opened?

Once you open the pack, immediately refrigerate and consume within 5 days. For Single Consumption pack, it needs to be consumed immediately after opening.

Are Demi's juices made from the best fruits?

Yes, every Demi’s Juice pack is made from the juice of the best farm-fresh fruits only. We do not use fruit concentrates.

What quality protocols does Demi's adhere to?

When it comes to quality, we adhere to the strictest protocols from sourcing to manufacturing and until the pack reaches you. We store our raw materials in sealed, tamper-proof containers in ideal temperatures and favourable conditions to retain freshness, taste, and purity. Complete automation and state of the art technology ensures strictly minimum human contact with the product at any time. Also, we abide by the strict guidelines on quality as outlined by FSSAI.
