Farm-to-Bottle Journey

Our unique 5-step process ensures consistent purity, excellence in taste, and 100% organic fruit goodness in every single pack.

01

Orchard Harvesting

Fruits are hand-picked at peak ripeness from sustainable, organic orchards to ensure natural sugar and nutritional levels are perfect.

02

Hand-Selection & Washing

Each batch is meticulously sorted by hand, selecting only the finest whole fruits, followed by thorough pure-water washing.

03

Pulp Extraction

Fruits are gently pulped using specialized state-of-the-art machinery to extract vitamin-rich juice and soft fruit meat. We never use concentrates or dilute with water.

04

Aseptic Packaging

Packed under sterile conditions in advanced European 6-layer aseptic boxes that lock out light and oxygen, guaranteeing long shelf life without refrigeration.

05

Emerging National Brand

Delivered directly to our superstockists and wholesalers nationwide, bringing 11 years of consistent purity and pure bliss straight to you.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I drink from this pack immediately?

Yes, but the best way to enjoy any Demi’s product is to serve it chilled. Also, always remember to shake the pack well before use.

How long can I use this pack once opened?

Once you open the pack, immediately refrigerate and consume within 5 days. For Single Consumption pack, it needs to be consumed immediately after opening.

Does Demi's Juice contain real fruit?

Yes, every Demi’s Juice pack is made from the juice of the best fruits only. We never use fruit concentrates.

Is this product tested for quality?

Yes, when it comes to quality, we adhere to the strictest protocols from sourcing to manufacturing and until the pack reaches you. Complete automation and state of the art technology ensures strictly minimum human contact. Also, we abide by the strict guidelines on quality as outlined by FSSAI.
