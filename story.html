Grown by Nature, Bottled with Care.

From day one, our mission has been simple: to bring the authentic, unadulterated freshness of orchard-ripe fruits straight to your table.

The Demi's Standard

We started in 2026 with a belief that beverages shouldn't be loaded with sugars, preservatives, or artificial chemicals. While other brands boiled away flavors with heat pasteurization, we committed to clinical cold-pressing and aseptic packaging.

Our packaging locks in nutrients and colors naturally, ensuring that every sip is as healthy and delicious as eating fruit fresh off the tree.

Sustainability at Heart

Our commitment doesn't end at taste. We pack all our products in FSC-certified sugarcane-derived paperboard cartons that are 100% recyclable, reducing our carbon footprint by up to 75% compared to plastic bottles.

We work directly with small organic farms across India to source premium Alphonso mangoes, Himachali apples, and fresh cow's milk, ensuring fair wages and eco-friendly farming practices.

Farm-to-Bottle Journey

1. Orchard Harvesting

Fruits are hand-picked at peak ripeness from sustainable orchards to ensure natural sugar levels are ideal.

2. Hand-Selection

Each batch is quality-sorted, washing away dirt and selecting only perfect whole fruits for juice extraction.

3. Cold Pressing

Fruits are cold-pressed to extract vitamin-dense pulp and juice without adding heat or dilute concentrates.

4. Aseptic Sealing

Packed under sterile conditions in 6-layer aseptic boxes that lock out 100% of light and oxygen.

5. Cold-Chain Delivery

Cartons are shipped in continuous cold-chain logistics to keep beverages nutrient-rich and fresh.
